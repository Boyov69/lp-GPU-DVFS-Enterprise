The Success Story

As an Armenian immigrant who has lived and worked in Belgium since 1999, I've experienced every level of hard work to build a better life for my family. My journey began with manual laborstarting as a garbage collector, physically loading garbage trucks by hand, then working as industrial cleaning staff. In 2010, I transitioned to taxi driving, and after 5 years of dedication, I became a private chauffeur for CEOs and board managers of major companies. Every job, every long hour was driven by one goal: giving my children access to the best possible education in the world. That determination is paying off beautifully. Today, I'm immensely proud of all three of my children: two have already achieved their academic dreams with Master's degreesone in Diplomacy and another in Social and Political Science. My third child, now 11 and still in high school, has been drawing like a professional artist since she was 6 years old. She aims for art academy, and I'm confident she'll earn her Master's degree in Art as well. Their success and potential gave me the courage to pursue my own transformation. When illness forced a career pause, it became the catalyst for complete reinvention. In just 5 months, I taught myself AI and built 8 working productsentirely self-funded, with zero external investment. This journey led to breakthrough GPU optimization technology that delivers 15-25% energy savings.
